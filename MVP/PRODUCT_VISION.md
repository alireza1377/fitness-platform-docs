# 1. Vision

Build an AI-first digital fitness platform that helps users achieve their health goals through structured programs, educational content, and measurable progress.

---

# 2. Mission

Enable anyone to improve their health with personalized guidance, professional support, and continuous learning in one platform.

---

# 3. Target Users

### Primary Users

- People who want to improve their fitness.
- People who want structured workout and nutrition programs.
- People who prefer learning before taking action.

### Secondary Users

- Fitness trainers
- Nutritionists
- Platform administrators

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Current fitness solutions are fragmented.

Users often switch between videos, workout apps, nutrition apps, messaging apps, and coaches without a unified experience.

This leads to poor consistency, low motivation, and weak long-term results.

---

# 5. Solution

The Fitness Platform combines:

- Structured Programs
- Educational Content
- Progress Tracking
- Professional Guidance

into a single integrated platform.

---

# 6. Product Principles

- AI-first
- Mobile-first
- User-centered
- Simple to use
- Data-driven
- Scalable

---

# 7. MVP Goal

Deliver a usable product where a user can:

1. Create an account.
2. Complete onboarding.
3. Receive a personalized program.
4. Watch educational content.
5. Track progress.
6. View achievements.

---

# 8. Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if users can complete their first program and continue using the platform consistently.

---

# 9. Out of Scope (MVP)

The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP:

- Marketplace
- Social network
- Live streaming
- Wearable integrations
- Advanced AI coaching
- Telemedicine

These features may be considered after validating the MVP.
